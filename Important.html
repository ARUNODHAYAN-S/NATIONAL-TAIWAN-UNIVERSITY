🎓 Scholarship Opportunities

National Taiwan University is committed to supporting students from all backgrounds with scholarships based on merit, need, and specific criteria.

  • MOE Taiwan Scholarship: Fully funded support for outstanding international students pursuing a degree in Taiwan.
  • ICDF Scholarship: Offers full scholarships including airfare, housing, tuition, and monthly allowance.
  • NTU International Student Scholarship: Partial or full tuition waivers based on academic excellence.
  • Research Assistantships: Provided by professors through funded projects for postgraduate students.
